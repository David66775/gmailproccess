Create Gmail Account

Step 1: Go to the Gmail Sign-Up Page

Open your browser normally and go to https://accounts.google.com/signup

Step 2: Fill Out the Form

Enter your First name and Last name.

Choose a Username (this will be your Gmail address).

Create a password and confirm it.

Click Next.

Step 3: Skip the Phone Number

If Google asks for a phone number, leave it blank.

If a Skip button appears, click it.
